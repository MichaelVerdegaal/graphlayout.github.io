Course overview

Graph layout algorithms, the practical way

Five layout algorithms — tree, circular, fractal, simulated annealing, and Fruchterman-Reingold — taught from an algorithms-and-Python angle, plus a lesson on how to animate any of them from a random scatter to the final layout. Maths shows up only where it earns its place. Each lesson has objectives, worked intuition, flashcards, and a knowledge check.
